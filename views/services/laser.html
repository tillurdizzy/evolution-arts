Laser Engraving and Cutting

Laser Engraving

Engraving Materials: Wood, acrylic, plastic, glass, leather, corian, fabric, coated metals, anodized aluminum, ceramics, mylar, pressboard and more.

Applications:
  • Promotional Products
  • Fundraising
  • Awards/Plaques
  • Decals
  • Barcodes
  • Gifts
  • Labels
Cutting

Laser cutting is a process in which a high-powered laser is focused and controlled by a computer to cut through various materials with higher precision and faster speeds, leaving a cleaner edge than many other cutting methods.

Cutting Materials: Wood, acrylic, plastic, delrin, cloth, leather, melamine, paper, rubber, veneer, cork and more.

Applications:
  • Vinyl lettering
